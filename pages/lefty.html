Lefty

Lefty's face is long and horsey with watery blue peepers. He's got a body like a scrappy pine: skinny and crusty. Also he has two left feet. He's wearing a ratty cowboy hat, torn jeans and a jacket, a backpack, a brown boot and a black boot (both left)

  • Role: Stalker
  • Mutations: Insect Wings
  • NPC to protect: Weezle
  • NPC to hate: Spitfire Hurst
  • Big Dream: Kill the mysterious beast of the forest

Patrick Perish

Lefty is played by Patrick Perish. Patrick has logged over 200 hours on Fallout and has read one or two science fiction books, so he feels qualified to be a part of a post apocalyptic role playing adventure. He is an avid roleplayer, getting his start from Dungeons and Dragons in 2016, and has been exploring the medium ever since.
