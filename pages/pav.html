Pavlovena

Pavlovena is 6 and a half feet tall. She wears a weathered and worn dog trainer's burlap suit. All that's left of it is a tube top of burlap, and the pads for the arms and legs. On the unprotected center section, she wears found sticky adhesive things (stickers, stamps, etc). She wears a big hot padded burlap suit frequently. She also has a dog companion named Crisp.

  • Role: Dog Handler
  • Mutations: (Wo)Man Beast
  • NPC to protect: Avian
  • NPC to hate: Brent
  • Big Dream: to find a better, less harsh life

Maggie Sotos

Pavlovena is played by Maggie Sotos. Maggie is a constant creator and contributor to fine arts. She’s written, directed, and acted in plays, musicals, improv performances, podcasts, and even web series video shorts. However, she is fairly new to the world of role playing, and is excited to be the “patient zero” to help relate to listeners that may also be new to this setting.

Other projects you can find Maggie in are the Grand Old Puppet Party web series and on stage in any of the Twin Cities improv theaters.
